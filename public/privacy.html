2. What personal data we collect and why

2.1 Account data

Full name, email, password (bcrypt hash — never plain text), account type, email verification status.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.2 Profile data

Optionally: city, country, bio, avatar image, social links. You control community visibility settings.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

2.3 Studio data

Studio name, city, country, description, tags, logo, your role, and operational records — kiln firing logs, task logs, event details, material purchases, cost summaries, attendance records.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.4 Sponsor data

Company name, description, category, website URL, logo, delivery countries, sponsor posts, and aggregate click and view statistics.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.5 Payment data

Payments are processed by Stripe. We store only your Stripe Customer ID, Subscription ID, status, plan tier, and period end date. We never store card numbers or CVV codes.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and legal obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) for accounting.

2.6 Communication data

Forum posts, replies, in-app notifications, and transactional emails (verification, password reset, 2FA, notifications). We do not send marketing emails without your separate consent.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.7 Security data

IP addresses (rate limiting on auth endpoints), 2FA secrets, login attempt metadata.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

2.8 Technical data

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers. Minimal server logs on Railway. Sponsor profiles receive aggregate view and click counts only.

3. How long we keep your data

  • Active accounts: for the duration of your account
  • Deleted accounts: personal identifiers (name, email, bio, avatar URL) anonymised immediately upon deletion
  • Deleted studios: operational data (kiln logs, tasks, costs, events, member records) retained for 90 days after studio deletion, then permanently purged. Inaccessible to all users during this period
  • Payment records: retained 7 years (German and EU tax law)
  • Server logs: up to 30 days
  • Forum posts after account deletion: displayed as "Deleted User" — content may remain visible

Media files (avatars, logos) in Cloudinary may take up to 30 days to be fully purged after account deletion.

5. Your rights under GDPR

  • Access (Art. 15): download your data via Settings → Security → Download my data
  • Rectification (Art. 16): update your profile in the app at any time
  • Erasure (Art. 17): delete your account via Settings → Delete account
  • Restriction (Art. 18): contact lelekstudio@lelekstudio.com
  • Portability (Art. 20): data export provided in JSON format
  • Object (Art. 21): contact us where we rely on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent: at any time, without affecting prior processing

We respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with:

6. Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • All communication encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Database hosted in Frankfurt (EU) with VPC isolation
  • Role-based access control — studio data isolated per tenant
  • Two-factor authentication (TOTP and email-based)
  • Rate limiting on all authentication endpoints

In the event of a personal data breach posing risk to your rights, we will notify the supervisory authority within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay (Art. 33–34 GDPR).

7. Cookies and local storage

The Aruru web app uses browser localStorage for your authentication token (aruru_access_token) and onboarding status — both strictly functional. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking, or analytics cookies.

8. International data transfers

Our database is hosted in Frankfurt (EU). Some sub-processors are US-based. For transfers outside the EEA we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs, Decision 2021/914) or the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where applicable.

9. Children's privacy

Aruru is not directed at children under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, contact lelekstudio@lelekstudio.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice at least 14 days before they take effect.
