Partners - from clay brands to retreats & galleries

Support our growing community - and grow with it.

Aruru stays calm and ad-free for ceramicists because the product is built around trust, not algorithms. Partnering with us means your name shows up where people already make real decisions - what clay to buy, which glaze to try, and which room they want to work in next.

That same feed welcomes pottery and ceramic retreats, residency programmes, and galleries looking for artists - not as noise, but as peers beside the studios and teachers our members already respect. You speak to an audience that is curious, skilled, and ready to travel for the right opportunity.

Where ceramicists
discover what to buy - and where to go next.

The partner directory is where studio owners and members look for suppliers - clay, glazes, tools, and books - with honest labels and real click stats. It is also a quiet window for venues and curators - list a retreat week, an open call, or a gallery seeking work - so the right artists find you without shouting over the feed.

Whether you ship materials, host makers for a month, or hang new work on the wall - you meet ceramicists inside Aruru, then they tap through to your site or booking flow. No dark patterns - just clarity.
